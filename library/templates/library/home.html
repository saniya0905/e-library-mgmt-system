Welcome to E-Library Hub, a modern digital library platform for students, librarians, and administrators. Access books online, manage resources efficiently, and enjoy an interactive learning experience.

About Us

E-Library Hub is a modern Digital Library Management System designed to simplify the way educational resources are accessed and managed. Our platform connects students, librarians, and administrators through a centralized digital environment where books, PDFs, and learning materials can be organized, accessed, and monitored efficiently.

By combining technology with education, we aim to provide a seamless reading experience that encourages learning, improves accessibility, and promotes digital transformation in libraries.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team focused on building innovative educational technology solutions that improve learning experiences and simplify resource management for institutions.

🎓 Student Focused

Empowering students with easy access to digital books, bookmarks, reading progress tracking, and personalized learning resources.

📚 Librarian Driven

Providing librarians with efficient tools to upload books, organize categories, and monitor student reading activities.

🛡️ Administration Ready

Enabling administrators to manage users, monitor system activities, and maintain a well-structured digital library.

What We Do

📖 Digital Book Access

Students can browse, read, and access digital books from anywhere at any time through a secure online platform.

🔖 Reading Progress Tracking

Automatically save reading progress and continue reading from the last viewed page without losing track.

❤️ Favorite Collections

Users can save their favorite books and quickly access them whenever needed.

📚 Library Management

Librarians can upload books, organize categories, and manage educational resources efficiently.

📊 Reading Activity Monitoring

Track student reading activities and monitor engagement through detailed reading history records.

⚙️ Administrative Control

Manage students, librarians, books, and categories through a centralized administrative dashboard.
